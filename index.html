About me

I'm, currently a freelance machine learning engineer working primarily for the Alan Turing Institute on the ML for Julia project (MLJ).

Previously I was a postdoc in adversarial ML at the University of Melbourne in Ben Rubinstein's group and before that in Yves-Alexandre de Montjoye's computational privacy group at Imperial College.

I received my PhD from Oxford in Bayesian ML. I was part of the OxCSML group, co-supervised by Arnaud Doucet and Yee-Whye Teh.
